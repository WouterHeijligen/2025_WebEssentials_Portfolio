Race report: record pit‑stop average and a decisive podium

Clean choreography, disciplined tyre program, and bold strategy calls.

Published: Dec 2025 • Categories: Strategy, Operations
Pit Crew Tyres Race Ops

Apex Velocity Racing delivered a clinical race execution, securing a podium on the strength of crew consistency and proactive strategy. A record average pit‑stop time across the event minimized losses under pressure and kept track position during key phases.

Setup and strategy

Pre‑race simulations indicated higher degradation on the medium compound; the team prepared for an aggressive undercut scenario paired with clean stops and flexible box timing. Double‑stack drills paid off when the safety car compressed the field.

Race timeline

  • Start: strong launch, settled into the leading group.
  • Lap 12: early stop to avoid traffic loss, undercut achieved.
  • Lap 28: safety car; clean double‑stack, maintained net position.
  • Laps 30‑45: fuel‑save windows aligned with ERS deployment plan.
  • Final laps: stable pace under pressure, podium secured.
“Smooth is fast—consistency beats isolated heroics over a full race distance.”

Operational excellence

Pit crew variance stayed within tight thresholds, and tooling accuracy reduced rework risk. Decision ladders provided clarity during safety‑car phases, preventing hesitation that often costs time.

Looking ahead

The team will carry these process gains into the next rounds, with targeted improvements to tyre warm‑up and race‑start procedures. Post‑event debrief actions are already scheduled with clear ownership and timelines.
