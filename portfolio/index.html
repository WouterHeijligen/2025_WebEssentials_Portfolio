About AVR

Apex Velocity Racing integrates engineering rigor with decisive race strategy. Our culture values transparent debriefs, repeatable processes, and continuous improvement.

From CFD and wind tunnel correlation to pit crew drills and telemetry pipelines, every domain operates on evidence, collaboration, and execution.

Read more

At Apex Velocity Racing, we turn complexity into advantage. Our workflow connects simulation, design, manufacturing, and trackside operations into one loop: insights from telemetry and driver feedback inform setup hypotheses, which are validated in practice and rolled back into development with clear owners and deadlines.

We organize around small, accountable squads—strategy, aero, chassis, data, and pit crew—so decisions happen fast and failure modes are caught early. Post‑session debriefs are candid and structured; we celebrate learning as much as results, because reliability and consistency are performance multipliers over a long season.

Our culture is hands‑on and human. We invest in cross‑training, safety, and well‑being, knowing that a confident, rested crew executes cleaner stops, sharper calls, and better engineering judgment. Continuous improvement isn’t a slogan—it’s the way we practice.

  • Data‑first decisions: clean telemetry pipelines, sanity checks, and dashboards tailored for rapid calls.
  • Correlation focus: CFD → wind tunnel → track validation to ensure upgrades deliver when it counts.
  • Operational excellence: drill cycles that target consistency first, absolute speed second.
  • Reliability as performance: robust parts, repeatable processes, and fewer DNFs translate to more points.
  • People & culture: psychological safety, clear roles, and transparent ownership of actions.
  • Sustainability mindset: smart freight planning, efficient manufacturing, and waste‑reduction initiatives.
