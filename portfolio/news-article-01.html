AVR announces multi‑year technical partnership

New collaboration accelerates materials R&D, simulation fidelity, and supply chain resilience.

Published: Dec 2025 • Categories: Factory, Partnerships
R&D Simulation Operations

Apex Velocity Racing has signed a multi‑year technical partnership designed to compress development cycles and deepen correlation across CFD, wind tunnel, and on‑track validation. The agreement spans materials research, composite manufacturing workflows, and data integration, targeting reliability and consistent performance gains across the season.

Scope and objectives

The program focuses on three pillars: (1) materials and composites with improved stiffness‑to‑weight ratios, (2) enhanced simulation pipelines that reduce delta between prediction and track data, and (3) operational resilience through smarter supplier logistics and inventory planning.

Development pathway

  • CFD campaigns linked to wind tunnel tests with clear acceptance gates.
  • Manufacturing feedback loop ensures designs remain practical under cost caps.
  • Telemetry pipelines feed back into design sprints for rapid iterations.
“Correlation isn’t a checkbox; it’s the backbone of development.”

What it means on race weekends

Improved tooling accuracy and part reliability reduce unexpected variance and costly setup changes. A tighter upgrade cadence allows packages tailored to circuit sensitivity, while simulation confidence enables more decisive strategic calls under pressure.

Next steps

The first deliverables will be evaluated during pre‑season testing, with follow‑up packages scheduled for early flyaway rounds. Post‑event debriefs will document correlation and reliability outcomes into a rolling playbook for continuous improvement.
